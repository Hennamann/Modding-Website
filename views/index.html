Welcome to my Minecraft Modding site

About Me

I'm from Norway and i am currently studying towards a career in multimedia(TV, Radio etc.). I enjoy programming and gaming in my freetime which has lead me to making Minecraft mods. I maintain several mods on my own like Mist Core/Biomes, Wasteland Reborn and NMCLC(NoMoreCapsLockCommands) But i'm also on the Built Broken Modding development team, working on mods like ICBM, Atomic Science, MilitaryBaseDecor and loads more!

Outside of modding Minecraft i like to make websites and web apps using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and NodeJS (All of which power this site).
